Te whare tapa whā

Our different aspects of our life that contribute to our well-being are interconnected, but it can help to consider different aspects of our well-being, so that we don't neglect an important part of our health.
In Te whare tapa whā our wellbeing is broken into 4 aspects: Taha Tinana , Taha Hinengaro , Taha Wairua , and Taha Whānau.

These for aspects of wellbeing, when cultivated, each strengthen one another, and are in turn built on the foundation of Whenua.

My plan...

Taha Tinana

  • - Do a rehab or finger exercise every break
  • - Walk my dog at least once a day
  • - Stretching 3x per week
Taha Hinengaro

  • - Go outside for at least 30 min a day

Taha Wairua

Taha Whānau

  • - Care for my animals each day
  • - Meet up with friends at least once a week
  • - Check in with people I know need it every week, or day if necessary
Whenua

  • - Practice mindfulness in a place outside that I feel at peace
  • - Feel connection to Earth and its inhabitants
