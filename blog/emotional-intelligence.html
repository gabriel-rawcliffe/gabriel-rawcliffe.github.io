Emotional Intelligence

What is it?

- The ability to recognise, process, and respond to your own feelings and the feelings of others.

- It is a skill that can be trained, practiced, and improved.

- It helps us to understand ourselves and our needs, as well as to work better with others

Why is it important

  • Performance: When we recognise and respond to our own feelings and emotions in an appropriate and healthy way, we gain insight and perspective that lets us perform better at tasks, study, and work.
  • Leadership and teamwork: Having hi EQ lets us build relationships with those around us and make better decisions. Makes you less of a dick
  • Happiness: By understanding what we are feeling and why we are feeling it, we gain perspective and are more easily able to move past self doubt and self flaggelation

How do we improve EQ?

  • Mindfulness
  • Conscious listening
  • Journaling
